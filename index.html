Your Mission

Work in pairs. Make choices, inspect problems, and prepare one realistic fix for your house model.

1. Spend Your Tokens

Choose how many of each electrical need or want your pair will power during a limited-electricity situation.

2. Inspect the House

Check each situation and decide if it shows danger, waste, both, or a safe setup.

3. Prepare Your Fix

Choose one inspection card, then modify or improve it for your own house model.

Provocation: Blackout Budget

Your pair only has 10 power tokens for the next 24 hours. Add electrical needs and wants to your cart, but stay within the limit.

Challenge Card: Normal Day

Everyone in the house wants to use electricity at the same time. Decide which needs matter most.

What will your pair power first?

Use the plus and minus buttons. You may choose more than one of the same item, but your total must stay within 10 tokens. Choose carefully: some items are useful, some are tempting.

Explore: Would This Pass Inspection?

Act as home safety inspectors. Classify each situation as danger, waste, both, or safe.

Your Pair Output: Reality Check Card

Choose one inspection card from the Explore part. Then improve or modify the situation so it can fit your own house model.

How to complete this card

  1. Choose one situation from the Explore inspection cards.
  2. You may use the problem as written, or modify it to match your house model.
  3. Create your own smart fix. Do not just copy the feedback.

After choosing a card, you may still edit the problem, type, and smart fix.
